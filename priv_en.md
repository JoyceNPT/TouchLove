**TouchLove**

**Privacy Policy**

Last updated: May 30, 2026

## 1. Introduction

TouchLove ("we", "us" or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect your personal data when you use the TouchLove platform and services.

By registering or using our services, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

-   Full name, email address, phone number
-   Account credentials: username, password (stored encrypted)
-   Date of birth, gender (optional)
-   Profile photo and personal profile content
-   Payment information (credit/debit card — only encrypted tokens are stored, never raw card numbers)
-   Shipping address
-   Messages, personal notes, and NFC-stored data

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

-   IP address and device information (browser type, operating system)
-   Activity logs: browsing history, access times, usage patterns
-   Cookies and similar tracking technologies
-   Approximate location (only when you grant permission)

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

-   Data from payment service providers (VNPay, Stripe, etc.)
-   Social login information if you choose to sign in via a third-party provider (Google, Facebook)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

-   Creating and managing your account, verifying identity
-   Processing orders, payments, and deliveries
-   Activating and managing the NFC chip in your product
-   Sending transactional notifications, order updates, and customer support
-   Improving features and user experience through data analysis
-   Sending promotional communications (only with your consent)
-   Detecting and preventing fraud and protecting system integrity
-   Complying with legal obligations

## 4. Sharing Your Information

### 4.1 Service Partners

We may share data with authorized partners (payment gateways, shipping providers, cloud services) to fulfill our services. These partners are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by competent authorities under Vietnamese law, or to protect the legal rights and interests of TouchLove.

### 4.3 Business Transfers

In the event TouchLove is acquired, merged, or transfers assets, your data may be part of the transaction. We will notify you in advance via email.

### 4.4 No Sale of Data

**TouchLove ABSOLUTELY COMMITS to never selling, renting, or exchanging your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.**

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

-   Data-in-transit encryption via HTTPS/TLS
-   Sensitive data encryption at rest (passwords via BCrypt, payment data via AES-256)
-   Access controls based on least-privilege principle
-   Continuous security monitoring and audit logging
-   Periodic security audits

However, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable regulations.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

-   Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data
-   Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
-   Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (unless legally required to retain it)
-   Right to Restriction: Request suspension of data processing in certain circumstances
-   Right to Object: Opt out of data processing for direct marketing purposes
-   Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise your rights, please contact: privacy@touchlove.vn

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

-   Essential cookies: required for the service to function (cannot be disabled)
-   Analytics cookies: help us understand how users interact with the service
-   Preference cookies: remember your settings and choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings or via the cookie banner displayed on your first visit.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal data according to the following principles:

-   Account data: for as long as the account remains active
-   Order and transaction data: minimum 5 years as required by accounting and tax regulations
-   Security logs: 12 months
-   After account deletion: personal data will be deleted or anonymized within 30 days, except where legally required to be retained

## 9. Children

TouchLove services are intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately and may deactivate the associated account.

## 10. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent in-app notification at least 14 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the current version.
